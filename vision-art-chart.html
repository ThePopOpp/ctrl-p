About the Vision Art Chart

The Vision Art Chart is a custom eye-chart art print that replaces the classic Snellen letters with the cities of your chosen state — a perfect blend of function-inspired design and local pride. Each chart follows the traditional Snellen acuity scale (20/200 down to 20/15) and is formatted for framing at standard print sizes.

Available for all 50 US states. Choose your state, customize city names, adjust typography, pick a frame color, and export a press-ready print file. Ideal for optometry offices, ophthalmology clinics, vision centers, and home décor.

  • State silhouette + city names at Snellen acuity scale
  • Fully customizable cities, typography, background & frame
  • Export as PNG (2400×3300 px), SVG, or press-ready PDF at 300 DPI
  • Printed on premium matte archival stock, ready to frame
  • Bulk pricing available for clinics ordering 10+

File Prep Guide

The Vision Art Chart configurator generates press-ready files directly — no design work required. Use the Export panel in the configurator to download your file in PNG, SVG, or PDF format.

Recommended export for print orders

Use PDF export for the most accurate print output. PDF is generated at 300 DPI and fits your selected print size with white margins if the aspect ratio differs. For lossless vector output, use SVG.

PNG export

PNG exports at 2400×3300 px (3× the 800×1100 canvas), suitable for most home printing services and digital display. Not recommended for large-format prints above 18×24.

Custom modifications

Need a custom layout, brand logo, or a size not listed? Contact us for a custom quote. We work directly from your SVG export.
