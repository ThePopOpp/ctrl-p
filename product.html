Built to last through any weather.

Our flagship vinyl banner is printed on 13oz scrim vinyl with UV-stable eco-solvent inks, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor use. The material is rip-resistant, waterproof, and engineered to hold vibrant color for up to 2 years outdoors and 5+ years indoors.

Every banner ships with reinforced hems and nickel-plated brass grommets positioned every 24" by default. Customize the grommet pattern, add pole pockets for street-pole installations, or opt for wind slits on large outdoor banners to reduce flag-effect in gusty conditions.

What's included

  • Full-bleed custom print on your choice of material
  • Nickel-plated brass grommets (standard) or custom pattern
  • Reinforced hemming on all four sides (add-on)
  • Free file proof within 4 business hours
  • 100% reprint guarantee on color or print defects

Design guidelines

  • · Add 0.5" bleed on all sides
  • · Outline all fonts to paths
  • · Embed all images at 150 DPI+
  • · CMYK color mode for print
