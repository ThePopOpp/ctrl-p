File setup guide

Follow these specs to ensure your yard sign prints exactly as designed, with no surprises.

Accepted file formats

JPEG or PDF only. Do not submit AI, PSD, or Word documents. PDF is preferred for best color accuracy.

Color mode: CMYK

Your file must be in CMYK color mode. RGB files will be auto-converted, which can shift colors unexpectedly — especially bright reds and blues.

Upload at final print size (24 × 18 in)

Do not add bleed or crop marks. Upload your file at exactly 24 × 18 inches. The print area is flush — edge-to-edge on coroplast.

Resolution: 150 dpi minimum

At 24×18 inches, your file should be at least 3600 × 2700 pixels at 150 dpi. Higher resolution is fine — max file size is 300 MB.

Double-sided orders require 2 files

Upload a separate file for Front and Back. Label them clearly. Both files must match the same size and resolution specs. Back artwork does not need to be mirrored — we handle orientation at press.

Quick checklist

  • JPEG or PDF format
  • CMYK color mode
  • 150 dpi or higher
  • Sized at 24 × 18 inches
  • No crop marks or bleed
  • Under 300 MB per file
  • 2 files if double-sided
Not sure about your file?

Our team will review your artwork for free before printing. We'll flag any issues before we run it.
