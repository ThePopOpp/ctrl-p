**Sizes:**

- 33"×78" (Standard)
- 36"×78" (Wide)
- 47"×78" (Extra Wide)

**Stand Types:**

**Graphic Options:**

- Glossy laminate

**Sizes:**
- 8ft Straight
- 10ft Straight

- 10ft Curved
- 20ft Straight / Curved

**Material:**
- Dye-sublimated polyester fabric (vibrant, wrinkle-resistant)

**Features:**

- Optional LED backlighting
