## Pricing Notes
- All prices above are starting prices for 1 unit, standard options.
- VistaPrint frequently runs 40–55% off promotions — their "regular" prices are rarely paid.
- Ctrl+P's prices are positioned slightly higher than VP promo pricing, reflecting local/custom service.
- Rush turnaround: add 30–50% to base price.
- File check/design service: add $25–$75 flat fee.
