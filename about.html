Our story

Born from a frustration with bad print.

In 2019, our founder Jeremy was running a digital agency and kept hitting the same wall. Clients needed signage, banners, business cards, and vehicle wraps. But getting those printed meant navigating clunky vendor portals, long turnarounds, and constantly nagging suppliers about file specs.

He wanted the quality of a high-end print house with the ease of a consumer shopping experience. When he couldn't find it, he built it.

Today, controlp.io combines direct relationships with the best print vendors in the country — 4Over, B2Sign, Max Pro Films — with our own in-house shop for specialty work like vehicle wraps and large format. Agency-grade design services start at $85. Same-day turnaround for local pickup. And actual humans on the phone when you need help.

We're not trying to be the biggest. We're trying to be the most reliable print partner in Arizona.
