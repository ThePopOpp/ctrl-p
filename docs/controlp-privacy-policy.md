# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: July 16, 2026**

ControlP.io ("ControlP," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and protect information when you visit [controlp.io](https://controlp.io/), create an account, request a quote, place an order, upload artwork or other materials, communicate with us, receive SMS or MMS messages from us, or use any of our print, design, signage, vehicle-wrap, custom-merchandise, installation, web-development, or related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy. This Policy does not apply to third-party websites, platforms, or services that maintain their own privacy policies.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect information you provide when you contact us, request a quote, create an account, place an order, complete a form, upload a file, join an SMS or email list, schedule an appointment, or otherwise interact with us. This information may include:

- Your name, company name, job title, email address, telephone number, billing address, shipping address, and other contact details.
- Account credentials and account preferences.
- Quote requests, order details, product selections, project specifications, measurements, installation details, deadlines, delivery instructions, and customer-service records.
- Artwork, photographs, logos, designs, text, files, brand assets, website content, and other materials you submit for a project.
- Payment, billing, tax, and transaction information. Payment-card details may be collected and processed directly by our payment providers rather than stored by ControlP.io.
- Communications you send to us by email, telephone, text message, chat, contact form, social media, or through a customer dashboard.
- Marketing and communication preferences.
- SMS or MMS consent records, including the date, time, source, method, form, campaign, telephone number, and status of an opt-in or opt-out request.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our website or online Services, we and our service providers may automatically collect certain information, including:

- Internet Protocol address, browser type, device type, operating system, language, and general geographic area.
- Pages viewed, links selected, referring pages, dates and times of visits, session duration, cart activity, and other interactions with the Services.
- Cookie identifiers, analytics data, and similar online activity information.
- Information used to detect fraud, protect accounts, diagnose errors, and maintain website security.

### 1.3 Information From Other Sources

We may receive information from payment processors, shipping carriers, fulfillment partners, identity or authentication providers, advertising or analytics providers, social-media platforms, referral sources, business customers, and other parties involved in providing the Services or completing a transaction.

## 2. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

- Create and manage accounts, orders, projects, invoices, payments, shipments, deliveries, warranties, and support requests.

- Protect the rights, property, safety, and security of ControlP.io, our customers, website users, service providers, and others.
- Establish, exercise, or defend legal claims and resolve disputes.

## 3. SMS and MMS Messaging Privacy

This section applies when you provide a mobile telephone number or communicate with ControlP.io by SMS or MMS.

### 3.1 Types of Messages

Depending on the consent you provide and your relationship with us, ControlP.io may send:

- **Transactional and customer-care messages**, such as quote follow-ups, proof-approval requests, order confirmations, payment notifications, project updates, appointment reminders, production or installation updates, pickup notices, delivery notifications, account notices, and responses to support requests.
- **Promotional messages**, such as print, sign, wrap, apparel, design, or web-service offers; new-product announcements; design tips; newsletters; and other marketing communications.

Consent to promotional text messages is separate from consent to transactional or customer-care messages. **Consent to receive promotional SMS or MMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any product or service.**

### 3.2 How You May Opt In

You may opt in through a clearly disclosed method, such as checking an unchecked consent box on a ControlP.io form, entering your mobile number in a designated SMS sign-up form, texting an advertised keyword, signing a written authorization, or making another affirmative request that clearly identifies the messages you agree to receive.

We may retain records showing when, where, and how consent was provided. An inbound message authorizes us to respond to that particular conversation, but it does not automatically authorize unrelated or ongoing promotional messages.

### 3.3 Message Frequency and Charges

- Promotional SMS or MMS messages may be sent **up to four (4) times per month**.
- Transactional and customer-care message frequency varies based on your requests, orders, projects, appointments, account activity, and communications with us.
- **Message and data rates may apply.** Your mobile carrier determines any charges.

### 3.4 Opting Out and Getting Help

You may withdraw SMS or MMS consent at any time by replying **STOP** to a message from ControlP.io. We also honor standard opt-out requests such as **END, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE,** or **QUIT** when received and reasonably identifiable. You may receive one final message confirming that your opt-out has been processed. After that confirmation, we will not send additional messages through that messaging program unless you provide new consent.

For assistance, reply **HELP**, call **(480) 999-9906**, or email **hello@controlp.io**.

### 3.5 Mobile Information and Consent Are Not Shared for Marketing

**ControlP.io does not sell, rent, or share mobile telephone numbers, SMS/MMS opt-in data, or messaging consent with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes.**

All categories of disclosure described elsewhere in this Privacy Policy exclude text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent. We may provide limited mobile information to vendors that act solely on our behalf to transmit messages, maintain consent and opt-out records, prevent fraud, or provide customer support, and those vendors may not use the information for their own marketing. We may also disclose information when legally required.
